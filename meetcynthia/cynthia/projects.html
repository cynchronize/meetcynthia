CrossTrack - A CrossFit Android Application

Project Vision
We aim to produce a creative design with the ease and fluidity of a seemingly simple application. With simplicity comes usability.

Our focus is on hardcore athletes who challenge their limits. Many progressive athletes keep records of their accomplishments. This application gives the athlete a way to view accomplishments while he or she is still at the gym. An accomplishment could be a better time in a 400 meter dash, or a personal best in the quantity of pull ups completed. Whatever the case, this application provides the ability to track personal bests and log them into your mobile device at the gym.

Technology Used
This application was written mainly in Java using the Eclipse IDE.

Project Team
CrossTrack was created by a team of 8 Computer Science students as part of the CSE110 Software Engineering course.

Muckraker News and Community Site
