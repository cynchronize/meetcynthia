My Interests

Web Design / Web Development

I have been experimenting with web design for nine years now, and I am still learning about all the cool things that can be made with the simplest tools. I love taking the time to make things visually appealing, as well as functional, and believe that first impressions are the most important. If your website or software application does not have am appealing user-friendly interface, the users will not be inclined to give the product a chance to demontrate its functionality and potential.

I use CSS3 and HTML5 for my web work and I am mostly self-taught in these languages.

Graphic Design

I enjoy creating and editing graphics in my free time. I mainly use GIMP 2 to create my works. I do not have very much experience with these tools and I am currently learning more through online tutorials and self experimentation.
